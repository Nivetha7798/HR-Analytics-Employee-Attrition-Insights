# HR Analytics-Employee Attrition Insights
Employee attrition analysis on 1,470 IBM HR records using Python, Pandas and Matplotlib to identify key workforce retention insights

## Overview
Analysed IBM HR Analytics dataset of 1,470 employees to identify key factors driving employee attrition

## Business questions answered
- Which department has the highest attrition rate?
- Does overtime lead to higher attrition?
- Do lower paid employees leave more often?
- Which job roles are most at risk?
- Does work life balance impact attrition?
- At career stage do employees leave most?

## Tools used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib

## Key insights
- Sales department has the highest attrition at 20.6%
- Overtime employees are 3 times more likely to leave(30.5% vs 10.4%)
- Employees who left earned $2,405 less on average
- Sales representative have highest role based attrition(40%)
- Poor work life balance(score 1) linked to 30% attrition rate
- Attrition peaks at first 2 years of employment
